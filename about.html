Combating Food Insecurity, One Kit At A Time: üì¶

We are Nom Nom: a student-run social-impact-based venture targeting food insecurity and food deserts in urban areas. Central to our ethos is the firm belief that anyone, regardless of race, gender, age, or socioeconomic status, deserves the right to have a stable and nutritious food source. Unlike food banks, community gardens, and communal fridges/pantries that require extensive volunteers and time in order to manage, Nom Nom offers the accessibility and efficiency of a self-managed and sustainable solution to the lack of fresh produce, right to your door.

Our solution? A low-cost, easy-to-use hydroponics gardening kit, that helps food insecure families grow their own fresh produce from the comfort of their home! Not only does our hydroponics gardening kit come with all the necessary materials needed to grow your own produce, it comes with educational resources and healthy recipes you can make at home with the plants you may grow!
